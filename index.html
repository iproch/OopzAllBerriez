a. Vision

Fury Bandits was created under the idea that NFTs will play a meaningful role in our future. Our goal with this project is to grow awareness and build tools that will make NFTs more approachable and easier to understand. Fury Bandits will accomplish this via two primary vehicles:

Bandit’s Heist - Bi-weekly community initiative to democratize the NFT space by redistributing up-and-coming projects to Fury Bandit owners

FuryPortfolio - An initiative to build infrastructure for the NFT ecosystem that will add value to NFT owners and prospective buyers. We’re building a publicly available tool with exclusive and free access for Fury Bandits owners as long as they own a bandit.

c. Who Are the Fury Bandits?

Owning a Fury Bandit will provide unique utility and opportunities to holders (banditos). The initial benefit will be the Bandit’s Heist.
We’re initially launching with the fury Otter but have designs in process for potential additional “characters” in the Fury Bandits universe
Will be launched on the Ethereum blockchain for an initial mint price of 0.08eth
